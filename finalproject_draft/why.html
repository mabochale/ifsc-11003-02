Why Do People Choose to do Vtubing?

When it comes to why, it depends on who you ask, but more often than not, it can be whittled down to 4 different reasons.

  1. Branding

    2. Some people might be ok with using their real identity on the internet, but may still choose to use a vtuber model instead because of personal preference.

  2. Security

    3. Meanwhile, on the opposite side of the spectrum, most people use a vtuber model due to wanting to protect their identity online while still basing their brand around an internet persona.

  3. Expression

    4. Most people use Vtubing as a fun means of self expression, which means that people can use Vtubing to help bolster one's creativity.

  4. Confidence

    5. As it would suggest, people use Vtubing as a means of helping improve one's confidence, which can have many positive effects.
