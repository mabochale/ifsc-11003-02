Frequently Asked Questions

How Much does comissioning a model cost?

Depending on the artist, Costs can vary, so be sure to research their prices!

What Software do i need? and are they free?

There are various ammount of software both for PNGTubing, and standard VTubing, most of which are Freemium, others are completely free. it truely depends on what your needds are.

Do i need a fancy computer?

While a high-end computer can enhance performance, many VTubers use mid-range systems effectively. It's important to check the requirements of the software you plan to use.

Can i VTube without showing my face?

Absolutely! Many VTubers choose to remain anonymous and use their avatars to express themselves without revealing their real identity.

Is Vtubing only for gaming?

No, VTubing encompasses a wide range of content including music, art, chatting, and more. It's a versatile platform for various forms of entertainment.

Do I Need a Camera?

While a camera can enhance your VTubing experience by capturing your facial expressions, some software allows you to use avatars without a camera, relying on voice or other inputs instead. Depending on the software, you can simply tether your cellphone to your computer and use it as a camera.
